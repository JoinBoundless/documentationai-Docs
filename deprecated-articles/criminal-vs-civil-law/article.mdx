---
title: Criminal vs. civil law
---
Cyber Sovereignty is intended to allow people who are freely agreeing to a new set of rules, to allow those rules to take precedence over all other rules. 

| **Consent**                                                                                                                                            | **Not consent**                                                                                                                        |
| ------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------ | -------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------- |
| Two boxers give their consent to engage in a regulated match where physical strikes are part of the agreed-upon rules and one sustains serious injury. | A person enters somebody's home when they're sleeping and attacks them.                                                                |
| Two people have a heated debate in a society that allows heated debates, and one of those two people feels offended.                                   | A person starts yelling using a loudspeaker outside the home of another person, on purpose, day and night, to stop them from sleeping. |
| A person records another person, without asking them, in a society that allows that.                                                                   |                                                                                                                                        |
| An individual provides informed, written consent to participate in a clinical drug trial after being advised of all known risks.                       | A researcher administers an experimental drug to a person without their knowledge or consent.                                          |
|                                                                                                                                                        |                                                                                                                                        |
