## **Furthering the concept**

### **Contract in the transaction screen**

Every transaction screen will utilize the AI brought to bear in each person's Synapse client to analyze the items being transacted and to come up with a list of questions that help detail the agreement between the parties.

Here are some example questions.

- Is this a sale or a license?Is the license for use worldwide?

- Is the item sold as is or are there specific warranties?

- Are there scenarios in which a refund would be entitled to the buyer? And if so, what are those scenarios?
