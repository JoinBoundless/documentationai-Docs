## Creating Documents

1. Login to your JoinBoundless account.
2. Select a project from your dashboard.
3. Click the "New Document" button in the sidebar.
4. Enter a title and content for your document.
5. Click "Save" to publish the document for your team.

## Editing and Organizing

- Use folders to organize documents
- Drag & drop to reorder
- Use markdown for formatting
