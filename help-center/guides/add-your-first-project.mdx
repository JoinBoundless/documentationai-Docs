## Quick Start Guide

<Steps>
  <Step title="Create a Project">
    From your dashboard, click "New Project". Give it a name.
  </Step>
  <Step title="Invite Team Members">
    Click "Invite" in your project's settings. Enter their email addresses.
  </Step>
  <Step title="Start Documenting">
    Click "New Document" to begin adding docs to your project.
  </Step>
</Steps>
