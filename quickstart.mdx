## Prerequisites

<Callout kind="info" title="Before You Begin">
Ensure you have:
- A valid email address for account registration
- Web browser (Chrome, Firefox, or Safari recommended)
- Optional: GitHub account for advanced integrations
</Callout>

JoinBoundless requires a free account to create documentation spaces. The entire setup takes under 5 minutes.

## Step 1: Sign Up and Log In

Follow these steps to create your account.

<Steps>
  <Step title="Visit the Dashboard" icon="monitor">
    Navigate to `https://dashboard.example.com` in your browser.
  </Step>
  <Step title="Register Account" icon="user-plus">
    Click **Sign Up** and enter your email, name, and password. Verify your email via the confirmation link sent to your inbox.
  </Step>
  <Step title="Log In" icon="log-in">
    Return to the dashboard and sign in with your credentials. You'll land on the main workspace overview.
  </Step>
</Steps>

<Callout kind="tip">
Save your credentials securely. Use a password manager for best practices.
</Callout>

## Step 2: Create Your First Documentation Space

Documentation spaces organize your project docs.

<Steps>
  <Step title="New Space" icon="plus">
    Click **New Space** in the top-right corner.
  </Step>
  <Step title="Configure Space" icon="settings">
    Enter a name like `My Project Docs`, select visibility (public/private), and choose a template if desired.
  </Step>
  <Step title="Create and Enter" icon="check-circle">
    Confirm to create. You'll enter the space dashboard automatically.
  </Step>
</Steps>

## Step 3: Upload Initial Documents

Add your first content using the editor.

<Steps>
  <Step title="New Document" icon="file-plus">
    In your space, click **New Document**. Name it `introduction.mdx`.
  </Step>
  <Step title="Edit Content" icon="edit-3">
    Use the built-in MDX editor. Paste or write your content.
  </Step>
  <Step title="Preview and Publish" icon="eye">
    Switch to preview mode, then click **Publish** to make it live.
  </Step>
</Steps>

Here's a sample MDX document you can use:

<CodeGroup tabs="MDX Example">
```mdx
---
title: Welcome
description: Your first document.
---

## Hello World

This is your JoinBoundless documentation!
```
</CodeGroup>

## Basic Interface Tour

Explore the key areas using these tabs.

<Tabs>
  <Tab title="Sidebar" icon="sidebar">
    The left sidebar shows your spaces, documents, and search. Use it to navigate quickly.

    - **Spaces**: Switch between projects
    - **Docs**: List of pages
    - **Settings**: Space configuration
  </Tab>
  <Tab title="Editor" icon="edit">
    Central editor supports MDX with live preview.

    Key features:
    - Syntax highlighting
    - Auto-save
    - Component auto-complete
  </Tab>
  <Tab title="Preview" icon="eye">
    Real-time preview pane shows rendered output. Toggle dark/light mode here.
  </Tab>
</Tabs>
