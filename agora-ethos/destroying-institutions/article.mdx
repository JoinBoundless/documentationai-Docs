# **A war of values**

Western society is undergoing a war of values. The right, seeing that the left had taken over the media and education, is now in full-blown war to either destroy institutions which propagate opposing ideas or hijack them. Each side of the political divide is vying for supremacy over the media, education and social platforms in order to turn them into spaces which impose their own side’s values to the determent of objectivity.

## **Unsurvivable chaos**

Many of these institutions are already unbelievably over-regulated and technologically backward. A partisan war to control them is the last thing they need for their survival.

## **Rejuvenation**

Cyber Sovereignty encourages digital societies everywhere to experiment with new forms of institutions:

- dedicated to the cutting edge

- dedicated to their art rather than to the political divide

- which put human life and freedom at their center.

Together we must reclaim the turf of our institutions becoming subject to a war of indoctrination.
