# **Agora's values: 10**

Note: The article refers to the Agora Genesis's ethos specifically.

## **Agora cherishes**

### **Human life**

- Agora believes human beings are endowed with dignity, equally, and that this exceptional dignity is the worthy focal point of its societal endeavors.

### **Truth**

- The pursuit of truth, wherever it leads, is the duty of every person.

### **Generosity**

- Service to humankind stands as the noblest response to the precious gift of life.

### **Excellence&#xA0;**

- The pursuit of excellence as the best instrument to serve humankind.

### **At the core**

Agora believes in a society driven to excellence through compassion.

## **&#xA0;Agora despises**

### **Complacency&#xA0;**

- Seeing a world in need and remaining inactive.

- The act of seeing values and rights eroded without speaking up.

### **Ignorance**

- The unwillingness to explore how one's own perspective may be limited by cultural, psychological or social factors especially the temptation to compromise the truth in order to fit in with the crowd.

### **Ruthlessness**

- The esteem of the effectiveness of a strategy or idea at the cost of morality.

### **At the core**

Agora despises complacency and ignorance as the enemies of effective compassion.
