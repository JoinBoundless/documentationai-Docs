# Agora's mission - 2 - Agora's basics

This article is part of a group of articles on **Agora's ethos**.

## **What is Agora?**

Agora is a digital society dedicated to the implementation of Cyber Sovereignty.

- In simple terms, Agora is a community organized online, dedicated to the proliferation of a political philosophy that maximizes human life and freedom.

## **Agora’s mission**

Agora exists to demonstrate the full potential of Cyber Sovereignty in community form.

### **Supporting objectives**

- Incubate and showcase Synapse
- Father new digital societies.

## **Agora’s pathway to impact**

- Gather individuals interested in its valuesMobilize them to:Create digital institutionsWork on SynapseCreate sub-societies (Demes) which split off into their own digital societies. Empower these digital societies to financially sustain themselves.

Agora is the first digital society of Cyber Sovereignty.

Agora exists to embody the mission and values of Cyber Sovereignty in community form.

Agora's primary purpose is to rally and mobilize the creators of digital societies.

It's a community of people organized online working toward a world dedicated to human life and freedom.
