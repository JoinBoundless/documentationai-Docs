# STUB: Continuing Western civilization

Agora harkens to an older version of Western civilization where:

- Progress was not seen as poison.
- Achievements were celebrated, not derided.
- History was something that made people appreciate and reflect rather than judge and apologize for.

Agora is for those who would like to opt out:

- Opt out of perpetual negativity
- Opt out of celebrating fragility
- Opt out of outrage as a currency
- Opt out of performative activism
- Opt out of cultural & historical guilt
- Opt out of medicated numbness
- Opt out of community replaced by consumption

Rather than apologizing for Western civilization, Agora sees itself as continuing the tradition of Western civilization.
