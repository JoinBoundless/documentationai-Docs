## **What will Agora become?**

As of spring 2025, Agora is a community hosted in Discord. However, its goal is to become an independent legal entity.

### **Agora's community roadmap**

Here is how Agorans envision evolving the community:

#### **1) Connect its members in ideal ways**

- Build partner matching systems  
- Create rating systems  
- Help members find work  

#### **2) Create a place to call home**

Agora needs its own independent space beyond Discord.

#### **3) Obtain independence**

The goal is full self-governance by the community.

#### **4) Fight for a new model**

Agora will push boundaries of what online societies can be.

### **Guiding principle**

This Polis must always be organized for its citizens, not profit.
