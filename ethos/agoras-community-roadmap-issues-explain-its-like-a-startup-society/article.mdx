## **What will Agora become?**

As of spring 2025, Agora is a community hosted in Discord. However, its goal is to become an independent legal entity that maximizes what a Polis can be.

### **Agora's community roadmap**

Here is how Agorans envision evolving our community toward its ultimate goal.

#### **1) Connect its members in ideal ways**

Today, the community is a hub of collaboration on Boundless projects. Prioritizing providing exceptional value to members allows the community to meaningfully reward engagement.

Examples:

- Building systems to match ideal project partners  
- Creating rating systems to evaluate collaboration quality  
- Helping skilled members find work opportunities  

#### **2) Create a place to call home**

To truly thrive, Agora needs its own independent space beyond Discord.

Any activism will face resistance, so Agora must be resilient financially, technologically, and legally.

#### **3) Obtain independence**

Agora is currently under the legal responsibility of Boundless but is designed to become self-governing.

The goal is true community governance.

#### **4) Fight for a new model**

Once independent, Agora will push boundaries of what an online polity can be.

It may advocate for:

- Its own currency  
- Community-based regulation  
- Rights for opt-in citizens  

### **Guiding principle**

This Polis must always be organized for its citizens, not profit.
